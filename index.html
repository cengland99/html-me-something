St Patricks Day Parade - My favorite

Our trip happened to be over St. Patricks day weekend. New York hosts one of the oldest and largest St. Patty's day parades in the United States. The parade runs up 5th Ave, and was only a a few blocks from our hotel. It runs for an incredible 5 hours.
